---
quote: "You are not your illness. You have an individual story to tell. You have a name, a history, a personality. Staying yourself is part of the battle."
author: "Julian Seifter"
context: "Identity & Illness"
---

When battling a mental health condition, it is easy to let the diagnosis consume your identity. This quote serves as a powerful reminder that an illness does not define you. You are a complete person with a rich history and unique traits. Maintaining a sense of self separate from the illness is a vital part of the recovery journey.
