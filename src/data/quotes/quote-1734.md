---
quote: "Prayer is sitting in the silence until it silences us."
author: "Richard Rohr"
context: "Silence"

We often think of prayer as something we do with our minds or mouths. But the highest form of prayer is allowing the silence to do its work on us, eroding the barriers of our ego until we are as quiet as the space we occupy.
