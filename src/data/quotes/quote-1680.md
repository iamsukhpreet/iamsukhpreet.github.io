---
quote: "If you have a glass, and you think 'This glass is already broken,' then you can enjoy it fully while it lasts."
author: "Ajahn Chah"
context: "Appreciation"
tags: ["impermanence","acceptance","buddhism","random"]
---

When we pre-accept the eventual end of an object or relationship, we are cured of the anxiety of losing it. This paradoxically allows us to appreciate its presence with a much deeper and more relaxed joy.
