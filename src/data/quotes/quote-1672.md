---
quote: "The bad news is you’re falling through the air, nothing to hang on to, no parachute. The good news is, there’s no ground."
author: "Chögyam Trungpa"
context: "Acceptance"
tags: ["impermanence","fearlessness","tibetan-buddhism","random"]
---

The groundlessness of existence is only terrifying if we are desperately seeking a fixed point of security. Once we accept that there is no 'bottom,' the fall becomes a flight, and uncertainty becomes a form of freedom.
