---
quote: "There is no such thing as a 'self-made' man."
author: "James Baldwin"
context: "Interdependence"
tags: ["humility","gratitude","interdependence","random"]
---

The myth of the self-made individual ignores the teachers, mentors, parents, and laborers who paved the way. Acknowledging our debts to others humbles the ego and naturally leads to a desire to give back.
