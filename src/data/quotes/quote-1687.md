---
quote: "Everything that is made beautiful and fair and lovely is made for the eye of one who sees."
author: "Rumi"
context: "Witnessing"
tags: ["impermanence","beauty","poetry","random"]
---

The world's beauty is made more poignant by the fact that it is transient. The sacredness of life lies in our capacity to witness and appreciate this fleeting splendor while we have the eyes to see it.
