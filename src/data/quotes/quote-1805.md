quote: "The times are urgent; let us slow down."
author: "Bayo Akomolafe"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["urgency","slowness","transformation","equanimity","random"]
---

In an era of accelerating collapse, the traditional response is to speed up, to fix, to react. Akomolafe suggests that slowing down is a topological shift that allows us to notice the new worlds sprouting in the shadows of the old. Equanimity here is the patience to witness what is truly emerging beyond our frantic efforts to control.
