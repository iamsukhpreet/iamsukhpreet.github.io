---
quote: "Life is a mystery, and it's best to live it as such—not as a problem to be solved, but as an experience to be had."
author: "Khalil Gibran"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["mystery","experience","life","awe","random"]
---

Gibran argues against the 'reductive' approach to life. When we treat life as a problem, we distance ourselves from it. When we treat it as a mystery, we enter into it, becoming one with the wonder we seek to understand.
