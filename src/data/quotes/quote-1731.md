---
quote: "Until I am essentially united with God, I can never have full rest or real happiness."
author: "Julian of Norwich"
context: "Stillness"
tags: ["rest","union","stillness","random"]
---

True rest is not a physiological state but an ontological one. We remain restless as long as we feel separate from the source of our being. Stillness is the practice of realizing that this union is already a fact, waiting to be felt.
