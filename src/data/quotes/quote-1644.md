---
quote: "The miracle is not that we do this work, but that we are happy to do it."
author: "Mother Teresa"
context: "Altruism"
tags: ["joy","love","altruism","random"]
---

When altruism is driven by love rather than duty, it becomes a source of joy. The 'miracle' is the transformation of what might be a burden into a spiritual practice that nourishes the giver as much as the receiver.
