---
quote: "Cut the desire for many things out of your heart and so prevent your mind being dispersed and your stillness lost."
author: "Abba Evagrius"
context: "Stillness"
tags: ["desire","focus","stillness","random"]
---

Stillness is compromised by the fragmentation of our attention. Every 'want' is a tether that pulls our mind in a different direction. By simplifying our desires, we gather our scattered energy back into a singular, peaceful point of presence.
