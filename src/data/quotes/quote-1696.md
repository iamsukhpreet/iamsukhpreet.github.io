---
quote: "I died as a mineral and became a plant, I died as a plant and rose to animal..."
author: "Rumi"
context: "Evolution"
tags: ["cycles","evolution","poetry","random"]
---

Rumi describes the soul's journey as an upward spiral of transformations. Each 'death' is seen as a necessary shedding of an old form to allow for a more complex and beautiful emergence.
