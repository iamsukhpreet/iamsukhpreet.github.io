If you are distressed by anything external, the pain is not due to the thing itself, but to your estimate of it; and this you have the power to revoke at any moment. Our suffering is rarely a product of objective reality, but rather the narrative we construct around it. By reclaiming sovereignty over our interpretations, we transform obstacles from paralyzing weights into neutral data points.
