---
quote: "I believe a leaf of grass is no less than the journey-work of the stars."
author: "Walt Whitman"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["wonder","sublime","nature","poetry","random"]
---

Whitman collapses the scale between the cosmic and the microscopic. In the 'sublime' view, there is no hierarchy of importance; the same atoms that dance in a supernova are present in the humblest blade of grass, making every inch of earth a miracle.
