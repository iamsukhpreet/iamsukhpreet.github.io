---
quote: "Trauma is not what happens to you. Trauma is what happens inside you, as a result of what happened to you."
author: "Gabor Maté"
context: "Psychological Insight"
tags: ["trauma","healing","self-connection","random"]
---

Trauma is the internal wound or disconnect from oneself that persists long after a stressful event has ended. Focusing on this internal response allows us to begin the work of reconnecting and repairing the self.
