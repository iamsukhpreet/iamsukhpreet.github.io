---
quote: "Death is a release from the impressions of the senses, and from impulses that make us their puppets."
author: "Marcus Aurelius"
context: "Peace"
tags: ["mortality","peace","stoicism","random"]
---

Marcus Aurelius views the end as a return to a state of profound stillness. It is a liberation from the constant, often exhausting demands of the physical body and the restless ego.
