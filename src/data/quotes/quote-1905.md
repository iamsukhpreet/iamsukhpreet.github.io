---
quote: "Nothing is too wonderful to be true, if it be consistent with the laws of nature."
author: "Michael Faraday"
context: "Science & Discovery"
tags: ["possibility","laws","nature","random"]

Nature’s reality often exceeds the wildest reaches of human imagination, provided it follows its own internal logic. We should never dismiss a possibility simply because it seems too magical or strange to our limited senses.
