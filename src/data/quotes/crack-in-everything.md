---
quote: "There is a crack in everything, that's how the light gets in."
author: "Leonard Cohen"
context: "Finding Hope"
---

Perfection is an illusion. Our flaws, mistakes, and vulnerabilities—our 'cracks'—are often what allow us to grow, learn, and experience grace. Instead of hiding our broken pieces, we can embrace them as the very places where hope and new perspectives enter our lives.
