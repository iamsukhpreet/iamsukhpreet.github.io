quote: "Everything that is, is because of everything else."
author: "Thich Nhat Hanh"
context: "Interdependence"
tags: ["interbeing","mindfulness","interdependence","random"]
---

Interbeing is the core of our existence. We are composed of non-us elements—sunlight, water, ancestors, and the labor of others. Recognizing this makes altruism a logical necessity rather than a moral obligation.
