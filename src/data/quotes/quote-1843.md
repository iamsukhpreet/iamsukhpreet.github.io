quote: "Too lazy to be ambitious, I let the world take care of itself."
author: "Ryokan"

A radical form of equanimity—the refusal to play the competitive games of power and prestige. By letting go of the ambition to 'save' or 'control' the world, we find a contentment that is immune to the world's failures.
