---
quote: "The sense of wonder is the mark of the philosopher. It is the beginning of wisdom and the end of dogma."
author: "Alfred North Whitehead"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["wonder","wisdom","philosophy","mystery","random"]
---

Whitehead positions wonder as the 'antibody' to intellectual stagnation. A person who is truly in awe cannot be a dogmatist, because they recognize that any system of thought is just a small boat on a vast, mysterious ocean.
