---
quote: "Be patient toward all that is unresolved in your heart and try to love the questions themselves, like locked rooms and like books that are now written in a very foreign tongue."
author: "Rainer Maria Rilke"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["unknown","patience","growth","poetry","random"]
---

Rilke invites us to treat our internal uncertainties with the same reverence we give to ancient mysteries. By loving the 'locked rooms' of our souls, we allow growth to happen at its own pace, rather than forcing shallow answers upon deep complexities.
