---
quote: "The ability to be in the present moment is a major component of mental wellness."
author: "Abraham Maslow"
context: "Psychological Insight"
tags: ["presence","mental-health","wellness","random"]
---

Anxiety often lives in the future, while regret resides in the past; mental health is found in the 'now'. Cultivating presence grounds us in reality and reduces the power of intrusive, ruminative thoughts.
