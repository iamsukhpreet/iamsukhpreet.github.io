---
quote: "Birth and death are only notions. They are not real."
author: "Thich Nhat Hanh"
context: "Reality"
tags: ["impermanence","reality","buddhism","random"]
---

From the perspective of deep meditation, the conceptual labels of 'birth' and 'death' are too small to contain the truth. Life is a continuous stream of manifesting and hiding, without a fixed start or finish.
