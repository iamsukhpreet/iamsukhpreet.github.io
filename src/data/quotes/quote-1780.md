---
quote: "The soul is no traveler; the wise man stays at home, and when his necessities call him into foreign lands, he finds the same mystery everywhere."
author: "Ralph Waldo Emerson"
context: "undefined"
tags: ["mystery","soul","presence","philosophy","random"]

Emerson suggests that the mystery we seek in 'exotic' locations is already present in the ground beneath our feet. The sublime is not a destination, but a state of mind that recognizes the infinite in the local.
