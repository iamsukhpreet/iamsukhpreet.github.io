---
quote: "It is better to enrich yourself through purity of spirit and to be without anxiety and then to speak."
author: "Abba Sisoes"
context: "Quietude"
tags: ["quietude","anxiety","speech","random"]
---

Meaningful speech must emerge from a depth of settled peace. If we speak from a place of anxiety or mental clutter, our words only add to the world's confusion. Quietude is the necessary preparation for any word that hopes to carry weight or light.
