---
quote: "We are dying every day; for every day a portion of our life is taken from us."
author: "Seneca"
context: "Time"
tags: ["memento-mori","time","wisdom","random"]
---

Mortality is not a distant event but a continuous process that began the moment we were born. Recognizing that time is constantly slipping away encourages us to value every hour as a precious, non-renewable resource.
