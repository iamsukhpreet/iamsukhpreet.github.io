The best moments usually occur if a person’s body or mind is stretched to its limits in a voluntary effort to accomplish something difficult and worthwhile. Growth is not found in comfort or passive enjoyment, but in the rigorous pursuit of challenge. Flow state is the reward for discipline—a cognitive alignment where the self disappears into the task at hand.
