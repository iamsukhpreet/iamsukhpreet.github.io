---
quote: "The world of men has forgotten the joys of silence, the peace of solitude, which is necessary, to some extent, for the fullness of human living."
author: "Thomas Merton"
context: "Solitude"
tags: ["solitude","modern-life","presence","random"]
---

In a culture that equates constant connectivity with importance, we lose the essential ability to simply be. True living requires a withdrawal from the collective noise to rediscover the texture of our own souls, which only reveals itself in the stillness of our own company.
