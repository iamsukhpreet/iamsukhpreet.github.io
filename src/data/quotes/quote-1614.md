---
quote: "If you have come here to help me, you are wasting your time. But if you have come because your liberation is bound up with mine, then let us work together."
author: "Lilla Watson"
context: "Interdependence"
tags: ["solidarity","interdependence","activism","random"]
---

This shifts the paradigm from charity to solidarity. It acknowledges that social structures affect us all, and true service is an act of mutual liberation rather than a top-down gift. Interdependence means my well-being is inseparable from yours.
