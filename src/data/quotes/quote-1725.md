---
quote: "My Beloved is the silent music, the sounding solitude."
author: "St. John of the Cross"
context: "Quietude"
tags: ["solitude","music","mysticism","random"]
---

Solitude is not a void; it is a resonance. When we are truly alone and quiet, the silence begins to 'sing' with a presence that is more fulfilling than any social gathering. It is the music of being itself, heard only when the instruments of the ego are stilled.
