---
quote: "Silence is something that comes from your heart, not from outside. It means that you are not disturbed inside."
author: "Thich Nhat Hanh"
context: "Silence"
tags: ["silence","inner-peace","mindfulness","random"]
---

True silence is a quality of heart that remains unshakeable. You can be in a crowded market and be silent, or in a quiet forest and be loud. The goal is to cultivate an inner sanctuary that no external noise can penetrate.
