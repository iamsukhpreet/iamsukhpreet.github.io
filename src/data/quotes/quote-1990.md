---
quote: "The 'shoulds' are the greatest enemies of a healthy mind."
author: "Karen Horney"
context: "Psychological Insight"
tags: ["self-compassion","expectations","mental-health","random"]
---

Living by rigid internal demands of how we 'should' be creates a deep sense of inadequacy and neurotic conflict. Replacing these demands with self-compassion allows for more organic and authentic growth.
