---
quote: "To begin depriving death of its greatest advantage over us, let us adopt a way clean contrary to that common one; let us deprive death of its strangeness, let us frequent it, let us get used to it."
author: "Michel de Montaigne"
context: "Mortality"
tags: ["memento-mori","courage","philosophy","random"]
---

Fear of the unknown is what gives death its power. By making mortality a regular part of our daily contemplation, we remove the shock of the inevitable and reclaim our mental power to live without dread.
