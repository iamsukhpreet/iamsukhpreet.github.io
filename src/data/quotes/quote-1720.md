---
quote: "If he is not edified by my silence, he will not be edified by my speech."
author: "Abba Pambo"
context: "Silence"
tags: ["presence","silence","teaching","random"]

The most profound teaching is often transmitted through our presence rather than our arguments. If the quality of our quiet doesn't inspire or heal, then our words—no matter how clever—will likely fail to reach the other person's heart.
