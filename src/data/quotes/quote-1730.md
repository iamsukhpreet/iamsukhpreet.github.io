quote: "Oh, when a soul is hid in Thee... Be silent. Be still in gentle peace."
author: "Teresa of Avila"
context: "Stillness"

There is a level of stillness that feels like being wrapped in a protective mantle. It is a 'hiding' that isn't about fear, but about intimacy—a state where we are so close to the source of life that the world's storms cannot touch us.
