Until you make the unconscious conscious, it will direct your life and you will call it fate. The path to wholeness involves integrating all parts of our psyche. Practicing this principle daily can lead to profound psychological shifts. In this 141st step of our journey, we find that self-awareness requires consistent practice and patience.
