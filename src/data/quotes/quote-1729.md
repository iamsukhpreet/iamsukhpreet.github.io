---
quote: "In the prayer of quiet, when the soul is at rest and the will is absorbed in God, the understanding and memory are also at peace."
author: "Teresa of Avila"
context: "Quietude"

Peace is not just the absence of conflict, but the integration of our mental faculties. In deep quietude, the intellect and memory stop their frantic searching and simply rest in the realization that they are already where they need to be.
