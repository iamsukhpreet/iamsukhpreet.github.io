---
quote: "Since death is certain but the time of death is uncertain, what is the use of anything but the Dharma?"
author: "Padmasambhava"
context: "Urgency"
tags: ["memento-mori","tibetan-buddhism","urgency","random"]
---

The extreme unpredictability of our final moment serves as a powerful call to action. It reminds us to prioritize our inner growth and ethical conduct today, rather than gambling on a tomorrow that isn't promised.
