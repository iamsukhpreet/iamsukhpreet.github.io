---
quote: "Go and sit in your cell, and your cell will teach you everything."
author: "Abba Moses"
context: "Solitude"
tags: ["solitude","wisdom","presence","random"]
---

We often travel far looking for wisdom that can only be found by staying put. The 'cell' is any space where we commit to remaining present with ourselves. In that persistent presence, the truth of our existence naturally rises to the surface.
