---
quote: "Love silence, my brother, for in it you have life for your soul. In silence you see yourself. Outside of silence you do not see except what is outside yourself."
author: "Abba Isaac"
context: "Silence"
tags: ["self-knowledge","silence","soul","random"]
---

Noise is a mirror that only reflects the external world. To see the landscape of our own interiority, we must turn off the lights of distraction. Silence is the light that reveals the soul's own features, which are otherwise hidden by the glare of the world.
