---
quote: "Without silence, we do not really experience our experiences."
author: "Richard Rohr"
context: "Silence"
tags: ["experience","silence","mindfulness","random"]
---

We consume events at such a high speed that we never truly digest them. Silence is the 'digestion' of life; it is the pause that allows an experience to move from a fleeting sensation to a lasting part of our wisdom.
