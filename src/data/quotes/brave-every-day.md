---
quote: "You don't have to be positive all the time. It's perfectly okay to feel sad, angry, annoyed, frustrated, scared and anxious. Having feelings doesn't make you a negative person. It makes you human."
author: "Lori Deschene"
context: "Emotional Acceptance"
---

The pressure to remain perpetually positive—often termed 'toxic positivity'—can be exhausting and invalidating. True mental well-being involves acknowledging and experiencing the full spectrum of human emotions without judgment. Embracing negative feelings is a healthy and natural part of life.
