quote: "To live in this world you must be able to do three things: to love what is mortal; to hold it against your bones knowing your own life depends on it; and, when the time comes to let it go, to let it go."
author: "Mary Oliver"
context: "Mortality"
tags: ["mortality","love","acceptance","random"]

Authentic living requires the bravery to deeply cherish things that we know will eventually fade. This acceptance of mortality doesn't diminish our love; it deepens our capacity for radical presence and affection.
