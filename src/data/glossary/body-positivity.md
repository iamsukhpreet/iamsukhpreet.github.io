<div data-lang="en">
The assertion that all people deserve to have a positive body image, regardless of how society and the popular culture view ideal shape, size, and appearance.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Body Positivity** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
