<div data-lang="en">
A common sleep disorder that can make it hard to fall asleep, hard to stay asleep, or cause you to wake up too early.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Insomnia** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
