Burnout occurs when you feel overwhelmed, emotionally drained, and unable to meet constant demands. As the stress continues, you begin to lose the interest and motivation that led you to take on a certain role in the first place.

### The Three Pillars of Burnout:
1. **Exhaustion**: Feeling drained and unable to cope.
2. **Cynicism**: Feeling detached or negative about your work or relationships.
3. **Reduced Efficacy**: Feeling like you aren't accomplishing anything.

Taking breaks, setting boundaries, and seeking support are crucial steps in recovering from burnout.
