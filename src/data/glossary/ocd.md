Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder is a pattern of unwanted thoughts and fears (obsessions) that lead you to do repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

### Why it matters:
Understanding **OCD** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
