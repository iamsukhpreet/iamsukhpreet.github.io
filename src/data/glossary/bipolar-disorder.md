<div data-lang="en">
A mental health condition that causes extreme mood swings that include emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression).

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Bipolar Disorder** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
