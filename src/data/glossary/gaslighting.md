<div data-lang="en">
A form of psychological manipulation in which a person or group covertly sows seeds of doubt in a targeted individual.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Gaslighting** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
