<div data-lang="en">
The physical, emotional, and mental limits we set to protect ourselves from being manipulated, used, or violated by others.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Boundaries** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
