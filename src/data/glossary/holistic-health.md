<div data-lang="en">
An approach to wellness that simultaneously addresses the physical, mental, emotional, social, and spiritual components of health.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Holistic Health** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
