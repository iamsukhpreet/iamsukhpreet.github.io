<div data-lang="en">

A defense mechanism in which the human ego defends itself against unconscious impulses by denying their existence in themselves while attributing them to others.

### Why it matters:
Understanding **Projection** is key to navigating mental health. It helps in recognizing patterns and seeking the right support when needed.
